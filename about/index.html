My Profile

I am a conservation biologist and I use population modelling to better understand and propose management actions for a variety of animal populations, from native Galapagos rodents to Malaysian orangutans.

Currently, my research focuses on the effects of environmental drivers of salmonid vital rates and consequences for their population dynamics.
